Instructions for the Interview

  1. Once you click "Start Interview" your camera turns on for capturing emotion.
  2. Next click "Start Detection" and start to record your answers for the questions.
  3. Once done, click "Stop Detection" to view the next page.
  4. You can check your results by clicking "View Results" button.
  5. Ensure you're in a quiet environment.
  6. Speak clearly and at a moderate pace.
  7. Be ready to answer the questions with confidence.
  8. Keep your answers concise and to the point.
